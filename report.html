Good afternoon Sugarloafers,

What a wonderful day at the King of Spring, these are the days that gave Sugarloaf this title. Although it was a little chilly at the start, it definitely led to some prime-time corn conditions this afternoon. With Mother Nature hitting the reset button for tomorrow you can be sure the spring vibes will continue at the King of Spring.

The grooming team tonight will hit a variety of terrain for the Sunday fun day shred fest. You can expect a few trails from the top of each lift with the possibility of a few more being thrown in there. Tomorrow we’ll continue our spring lift operations with Skyline, SuperQuad, and Double Runner.

The weather tonight calls for increasing clouds and temperatures dropping to around freezing. Tomorrow warms up quicker than today as temps hit a high in the low 50s around the base for Sunday. A few clouds will be around but expect mostly sunny skies with winds chillin’ in the 20-30mph range.

 

Cheers,

Chris
