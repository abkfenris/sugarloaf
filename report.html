After a blustery morning things have calmed down quite a bit here, and with bright blue skies and comfortable temps, it's turned into a pretty pleasant afternoon. SuperQuad and Skyline both opened up around lunch time, and Whiffletree wasn't too far behind, giving us access to most of the mountain and plenty of terrain. 

If you ended up sitting today out, fear not. Tomorrow is looking like another nice one, with temps reaching to near 40 degrees and minimal winds throughout the day. And after last night's late freeze, the grooming team will have a full night tonight to whip everything back into shape, meaning surface conditions should be considerably more enjoyable first thing tomorrow morning. 

For lifts, look for the full roster to operate tomorrow morning, with the exception of Timberline, which will not run tomorrow. Winds don't look like they should be much of an issue for us tomorrow, but as always, it's a good idea to check in right here before you head up. 

We're looking at cloudy skies for much of the day tomorrow, but temps will be climbing throughout the day, eventually reaching the upper 30s at the base by the afternoon. 

Ethan
