Good morning Sugarloafers,

The Sunday fun day Is here for the taking. So make that first chair, ski that double black, or find a new place on the mountain to just relax and take it all in. With the corn cycle and nice weather continuing into tomorrow, a good choice could be to just book the extra night and enjoy another day of snow riding at the King of Spring.

The grooming team took to the hill and hit the usual late-season list around the central mountain. Make sure to check below for this morning’s grooming report. Please be advised when melting snow freezes overnight it leaves off-piste terrain hard and dangerous to ski, so sometimes Patrol will opt to close the terrain for a couple of hours until it corns up nicely from warm temps and sun. Our Spring Lift Operations continue on today with SuperQuad, Skyline, and Double Runner.

The weather today calls for mostly sunny skies with high temperatures reaching the low 50s around the base. Winds are calm today with speeds ranging from 15-20mph. This morning temperatures Top of Skyline were reported at 21 degrees so make sure to wear and extra layer this morning and anticipate some fast snow riding conditions.

Cheers,

Chris
