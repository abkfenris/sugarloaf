Good afternoon Sugarloafers,

 The Skiing and riding today exceeded all expectations and delivered the goods for a beautiful morning. With the holidays behind us and 2022 in its infancy, we can slip back into our comfortable routines and  continue working towards our goals. For the next 4 months, our lifts will be turning. So when you’re in the middle of the grind and feeling like you need a break. Always remember the fresh open air of the western Maine Mountains is only a car ride away.

With temperatures in the brrr category tonight the snowmakers will take advantage of ideal conditions and maintain operations on Haulback, Stomping Grounds, and Upper Tote Road. Check out our Facebook or Instagram page to scope a photos of the piles that are being made currently. The grooming team will hit the hill in force tonight and groom everything from Windrow to Widowmaker.

Cold temps will persist into tonight with around 3 degrees at the base and dropping to below zero at the summit. Tonight’s winds remain pretty chill in the 15-20mph range and continue into tomorrow, which looks to be another glorious sunny day of skiing and riding with temps slightly warmer in the mid-teens. Looking ahead we have a small system that will affect us starting Wednesday, which looks to be mostly light snow and flurries. More on that tomorrow morning when things come a little more into focus.

 

Have a great night,

Chris

Uphill Access
During Christmas Vacation week (Saturday, December 25 thru Sunday, January 2), uphill travel will be limited to Windrow only and will not be allowed any higher than Bullwinkle’s. Uphill skiers can begin climbing as usual at 7:00am, with downhill travel allowed anytime after 8:30am. For additional information on uphill travel, please visit sugarloaf.com/uphill-access.
