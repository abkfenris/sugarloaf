Good morning, Sugarloaf skiers and riders! Here’s what those heading to the hill this Saturday, January 8, can expect in terms of conditions and terrain: we added Haulback to our tally yesterday, bringing our total tally  to 45  trails–that’s 271 open acres for you to explore in your on-hill adventures today.

Temperatures will remain in teens, paired with sunny skies and elevated winds up to 50 miles per hour, which pend the possibility of wind holds; as always, we’ll keep you updated right here on the Mountain Report as well as our Snow Reporting Twitter, @SugarloafSnow, with the latest.

Overnight our tireless team of Sugarloaf groomers headed to the hill to smooth out all of our open terrain, getting is ready, set, prepped for today’s shredfest. In other operational news, our snowmakers got the gins going on family-favorite Scoot yesterday; next, their plan is to fire things up in Sidewinder and over on West Mountain. And word on the Terrain Park scene says Stomping Grounds should open Monday!

For those planning their afternoon après, be sure to join us for Road to Reggae with the Northeast Dub Allstars, presented by Baxter Outdoors, happening 3-7pm in The Widowmaker.
