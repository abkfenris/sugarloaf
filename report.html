Good morning Sugarloafers,

We’ve made it past another hump day and are truckin’ towards another weekend of snow riding, eating, dancing, and laughing. Several acts of entertainment are lined up for this weekend including Bob Marley and The Ghost of Paul Revere. With the soft snow persisting from recent storms. It is surely going to be another memorable weekend of leisure here at Sugarloaf.

Friday things look to clear up for a sunny day with continued mild temperatures and moderate winds. After a weak disturbance Saturday, things appear to be dry until late next week, which brings a chance of statewide precipitation.

Safe Travels,
